Deploy and manage jobs in DC/OS

Usage:
  dcos job [command]

Available Commands:
  add         Add a job
  history     Provides a job run history
  kill        Kill a job
  list        Show all job definitions
  queue       Show job runs that are queued
  remove      Remove a job
  run         Run a job now
  schedule    Manage schedules of jobs
  show        Show a job
  update      Update a job

Flags:
  -h, --help   help for job

Use "dcos job [command] --help" for more information about a command.
