Deploy and manage jobs in DC/OS

Usage:
    dcos job [command]

Commands:
    add
        Add a job
    history
        Provides a job run history
    kill
        Kill a job
    list
        Show all job definitions
    queue
        Show job runs that are queued
    remove
        Remove a job
    run
        Run a job now
    schedule
        Manage schedules of jobs
    show
        Show a job
    update
        Update a job

Options:
    -h, --help
        help for job

Use "dcos job [command] --help" for more information about a command.
